Experiments 1 and 2


Confidence scale: subjects pressed a key with their left hand between 1 (very unsure) to 6 (very sure).

Feedback: none

Subj_idx: the subject number
Stimulus: stimulus identity (1 ~ TOJ left first, 2 ~ TOJ right first)
Response: subject's response (1 ~ left, 2 ~ right)
Confidence: subject's confidence rating (between 1 and 6)
RT_decision: the decision reaction time (RT in seconds)
RT_confidence: the confidence reaction time (RT in seconds)
Ease: stimulus energy difference between right and left

Subject population: EPFL students (18-35)

Response device: keyboard

Experiment setting: experimental booth with a robot inducing sensorimotor conflicts

Training: 10 min prior to the experiment
